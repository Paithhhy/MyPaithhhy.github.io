About Me

Hello! My name is Jose Dimitri Cubillo, and I am a dedicated and passionate individual pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Holy Name University. I hail from the picturesque town of Basdacu, Loon, Bohol, where I developed a deep appreciation for technology and its potential to transform lives. As an IT student, I am constantly seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills in various areas of information technology, including programming, networking, database management, and cybersecurity. I am particularly interested in web development and software engineering, as I enjoy creating innovative solutions that enhance user experiences. In addition to my academic pursuits, I actively participate in extracurricular activities related to technology. I am a member of the university's IT club, where I collaborate with fellow students on projects and attend classes to stay updated with the latest industry trends. Outside of academics, I have a keen interest in graphic design. I believe that creativity plays a crucial role in the field of IT, and I strive to incorporate artistic elements into my work whenever possible. In my free time, I enjoy exploring new places, capturing moments through my beloved eyes, and experimenting with design software to create visually appealing graphics. Overall, I am committed to continuous learning and growth in the field of information technology. I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead and look forward to contributing my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact in the tech industry.
